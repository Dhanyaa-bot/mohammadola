SS Products

Macamet’s stainless steel (SS) products are crafted with precision to offer strength, corrosion resistance, and aesthetic appeal. Our SS solutions cater to industries requiring robust yet visually refined materials, such as construction, food processing, and healthcare. Our stainless steel products are designed for high performance in challenging environments. With excellent resistance to rust, heat, and chemicals, they ensure long-term durability, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Stainless Steel Gratings

Macamet offers a comprehensive range of Stainless Steel (INOX) grating, designed for applications where rust and corrosion resistance are critical. We handle the entire process, from design and layout specifications to manufacturing, quality control, and delivery. Stainless Steel grating is typically custom-manufactured to meet specific project requirements. Standard panel sizes align with steel grating stock panels, depending on the selected profile. Types of Stainless Steel Grating:

  • Top Notch Grating
  • Pass Thru Grating
  • Press Lock Grating

Stainless Steel Access Cover

Macamet manufactures high-quality Stainless Steel Access Doors, designed for easy access to walls or ceilings for maintenance and emergency purposes. Available in flush or recessed types, we offer both standard sizes and custom-made solutions as per client requirements. Specifications:

  • Type: Flushed / Recessed / Hinged
  • Material: Stainless Steel (AISI 304, 304L, 316, 316L)
  • Finish: Mill Finish

SS Floor drains & Floor trap

Macamet offers a wide range of stainless steel drainage solutions, including square drains, tile drains, and custom-made drains. Our drains are designed to effectively remove standing water, making them ideal for low water flow rate applications such as bathrooms, kitchens, and industrial spaces. Types of Stainless Steel Drains:

  • Stainless Steel Floor Drains
  • Stainless Steel Cleanouts
  • Stainless Steel Floor Traps
  • Stainless Steel Roof Drains
  • Stainless Steel Water Outlets
  • Superior corrosion resistance

Stainless Steel Fabrication

At Macamet, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality stainless steel fabrication services, supporting clients operating in challenging and remote environments. Our mission is to provide cost-effective solutions with minimal environmental impact while maintaining uncompromised quality.

SS Linear drain & Shower Drains

Macamet offers a wide range of stainless steel drains, including square drains, linear drains, recessed drains, solid top drains, tile drains, and custom-made drainage solutions. Our drains are designed for efficient water removal, ensuring durability and corrosion resistance across various applications. Types of Stainless Steel Drains:

  • Stainless Steel Square Drains
  • Stainless Steel Linear Drains

Stainless Steel Tactile Indicators

Macamet offers a range of Stainless Steel (S.S) Tactile Indicators designed as per BS EN 10088-1 standards. These indicators help create a safer environment for the blind and visually impaired, providing essential guidance on pedestrian pathways. Once installed, they alert individuals to potential hazards or directional changes ahead. Types of Macamet Stainless Steel Tactile Indicators:

  • Type 1 – Decision Stud (Circular Type)
  • Type 2 – Directional Strips (Rectangular Type)

Stainless Steel Manhole Cover

Macamet designs and manufactures Stainless Steel (SS) Manhole Covers in compliance with BS EN 124 standards. These covers are categorized into six classes, from A15 (Light Duty) to F900 (Ultra-Heavy Duty), with the most commonly used ranging from A15 to D400. Categories of SS Manhole Covers:

Common Sizes 300x300 mm / 450x450 mm / 600x600 mm / 900x900 mm - C/O
Thickness As per Specification
Type Recessed S.S Manhole Cover / Solid Top S.S Manhole Cover
Type of Seal Single / Double
Load Type Light / Medium / Heavy Duty / Ultra Heavy Duty
Material Stainless Steel AISI 304 / 304L / 316 / 316L Grade
Finish Mill Finish / Brush Finish / Mirror Finish
