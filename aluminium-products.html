Aluminum Products

Our aluminum products are valued for their lightweight properties, corrosion resistance, and versatility. Suitable for structural frameworks, cladding, and industrial equipment, our aluminum solutions offer strength without adding unnecessary weight. Macamet’s aluminum products are engineered for exceptional durability, even in challenging conditions. Whether for construction projects, automotive applications, or custom designs, our aluminum solutions combine flexibility with lasting performance.

Aluminum Handrails

Macamet manufactures high-quality Aluminum Handrails, ideal for use in highly corrosive environments. These handrails are lightweight, durable, and aesthetically pleasing, making them a preferred choice for various applications. Available in both welded and modular options, our handrails ensure long-term reliability with minimal maintenance. Available Aluminum Grades:

  • 6063 / 6065 / 6082
  • Special alloys available upon request

Aluminium Gratings

Macamet specializes in high-quality Swaged and Press-Locked Bar Grating, designed for durability and high load-bearing capacity. We offer Swaged Bar Grating in Aluminum and Stainless Steel, with custom manufacturing available for Carbon Steel upon request.Macamet Aluminum Grating is:

  • Corrosion-resistant, non-sparking, and rustproof
  • Lightweight yet strong, making it ideal for industrial applications
  • Available in standard or anodized finishes

Aluminum Ladders

Macamet Aluminum Ladders are designed for safety, durability, and ease of installation, making them the preferred choice worldwide across various industries. Engineered for low maintenance and long service life, our ladders ensure high reliability in challenging environments.

  • High Corrosion Resistance – Ideal for both internal and external applications
  • Lightweight & Durable – Easy to install and transport
  • Reliable & Safe – Meets industry safety standards
  • Available in Standard & Custom Sizes

Aluminium Access Covers

Macamet manufactures high-quality Access Hatch Covers designed for roofs, floors, and water tank enclosures. Our hatch covers are available in standard sizes and can also be custom-made to meet specific project requirements.

  • Roof Type – Ideal for safe rooftop access
  • Up-Stand Type – Raised edges for added protection
  • Flushed Type – Seamless integration with the surface
  • Glazed Type – Incorporates glass for enhanced visibility
  • Drop-in Type – Easy installation with a drop-in design

Aluminium Gangways

Macamet provides premium-quality Aluminium Gangways, designed to be lightweight, durable, and aesthetically pleasing. Our gangways are maintenance-free and cost-effective, ensuring maximum usability in various environments.

  • Ideal for Marine & Offshore Applications – Our Wharf Ladders & Gangways are widely used in ports, harbors, offshore platform
  • Low Maintenance – No rusting, no painting required
  • Safe & Secure – Designed for stability and load-bearing efficiency
  • industrial facilities

Aluminium Manhole Covers

Macamet manufactures high-quality Aluminium and Stainless Steel Manhole Covers, widely used in shopping malls, commercial complexes, residential units, and industrial facilities. Our manhole covers are designed to be durable, corrosion-resistant, and available in various standard and custom sizes.

  • Roof Type, Up-Stand Type
  • Flushed Type, Glazed Type
  • Drop-in Type

Aluminum Access Platforms

Macamet Aluminium Access Platforms provide safe and reliable access to critical areas in various industries. Our platforms are engineered for durability, corrosion resistance, and easy installation, making them ideal for external and internal applications.

Understanding that every project has unique requirements, Macamet provides custom-made Aluminium Access Platforms tailored to specific needs. Whether for industrial, marine, or power sector applications, our expert team works closely with clients to design and deliver solutions that enhance safety and accessibility.
