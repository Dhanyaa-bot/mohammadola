Steel Products

Macamet offers a wide range of high-quality steel products designed to meet the demands of various industries. Our steel solutions are known for their exceptional strength, durability, and resistance to harsh environmental conditions. From structural components to customized steel fittings, our products ensure superior performance in construction, infrastructure, and industrial applications. We prioritize precision manufacturing to deliver steel products that align with stringent quality standards. Whether for heavy-duty frameworks, pipelines, or specialized steel structures, our solutions provide unmatched reliability, supporting both safety and long-term functionality.

Electro Forged Steel Grating

Electro-Forged Gratings are the most widely used grating type due to their superior strength, durability, cost-effective production, and ease of installation. They are commonly found in industrial facilities and architectural structures, serving applications such as pedestrian walkways, platforms, safety barriers, drainage covers, and ventilation grates. Specifications:

  • Load Bar Depth: 25mm - 125mm
  • Load Bar Thickness: 3mm, 4mm, 4.5mm, 5mm, 6mm, 8mm, 10mm
  • Load Bar Type: Plain or Serrated
  • Cross Bar Size: Twisted square bar – 6mm, 8mm, 10mm
  • Pitch / Mesh Sizes: 30x100mm, 30x50mm, 41x100mm, 41x50mm, 34x38mm, 35x75mm, 35x100mm
  • Finish Options: Black self-color, painted, or hot-dip galvanized

Press-Locked Grating

Press-locked steel bar gratings are designed with an interlocking system of perpendicular bars, providing a strong and stable structure. These gratings are widely used for flooring platforms supporting vehicle movement, industrial walkway access systems, and architectural applications such as grilles, dividers, and screens.

  • Load Bar Sizes: 25mm – 150mm
  • Load Bar Thickness: 3mm – 6mm
  • Serrated: Slip-resistant for enhanced safety
  • Plain: Smooth surface for general use

Heavy Duty Gratings

We provide heavy-duty gratings with bearing bar heights of up to 120 x 10 mm, capable of handling up to 80 tons of uniformly distributed load (UDL) and 11.5 tons of concentrated load with an approximate clear span of 1 meter. Load tables can be referenced to assist in selecting the appropriate load bars based on specific requirements.

Manually welded heavy-duty gratings are engineered to endure both rolling and static loads, making them suitable for highways, plant floors, loading docks, inlet covers, and airports. These gratings accommodate a range of conditions, from light-duty forklifts to fully loaded semi-trucks and trailers meeting maximum highway load limits.

Steel Stair Treads

Each stair tread is supplied with end plates featuring slotted holes for easy installation. Alternatively, stair treads can be manufactured without end plates upon request. By default, stair treads come with a slip-resistant pyramid nosing bar to enhance safety. Other nosing options are available upon request to meet specific requirements.

  • Width: 250mm, 280mm
  • Length: 800mm, 1000mm, 1200mm
  • Custom sizes available upon requesty

Grating Clamps

Macamet specializes in the manufacturing and supply of Grating Fixing Clamps in Mild Steel with a Hot-Dip Galvanized Finish, designed for securing mild steel galvanized gratings. For specialized applications, we offer Stainless Steel AISI 304 or 316 Clamps, ideal for securing stainless steel (S.S.), aluminum (Al.), and FRP/GRP gratings.

  • Pressed Clamp Plate (Bottom Plate)
  • M-Type Saddle Clip
  • M8 x 70mm Bolt and Nut

Steel Handrails

Our welded handrails are fabricated according to client specifications, with vertical handrail posts, horizontal top and mid rails, and toe or kick plates securely welded together before painting or galvanizing for durability. We provide fully fabricated handrail systems commonly used in walkways, staircases, and storage tanks. Applications:

  • Airports
  • Sewage Treatment Plants
  • Desalination Plants
  • Oil & Gas Sector and more
