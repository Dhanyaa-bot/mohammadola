Fencing Products

Our fencing products provide effective security solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial sites. Designed for strength and durability, our fencing systems are ideal for perimeter protection, restricted areas, and controlled access points. Macamet’s fencing solutions are available in various materials and designs to suit different requirements. Engineered for easy installation and long-term performance, our fencing products ensure enhanced security without compromising aesthetics.

Welded Wire Mesh

Macamet Galvanized Welded Mesh Panels are crafted from high-quality welded wire mesh, offering a flat, even surface and robust structure for a wide range of applications, including construction, food processing, agriculture, and industrial use. The corrosion-resistant coating ensures long-lasting durability in harsh environments. Macamet ensures top-quality welded mesh solutions for KSA and beyond, meeting the highest industry standards for performance and reliability.

  • Hot-dip galvanized after welding
  • Electro galvanized after welding
  • PVC coated before welding
  • Stainless Steel welded wire mesh
  • Crimpled wire mesh
  • Woven wire mesh

Steel Hoarding Fence

Macamet Steel Hoarding offers enhanced site security and serves as a sturdy alternative to traditional timber hoarding. Designed for construction sites, event spaces, and restricted areas, our hoarding panels provide privacy, safety, and durability.

Macamet supplies high-security razor wire fencing for perimeter protection in sensitive areas. Available in:

  • Straight-line or Crossed Concertina Coils
  • Barbed Tape Concertina (BTC) & Barbed Tape Obstacle (BTO)
  • Material Options: Galvanized or Stainless Steel
